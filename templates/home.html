About Us

We strive to create a dynamic, open-minded environment that nurtures the academic and creative growth of the electrical engineering student body, and provide a welcoming place for students to meet and socialize.

Our Mission

As the liaison between students and the faculty, we help facilitate an education that harmonizes with both student interest and industries of Alberta.

Our Club

Refreshments

Ranging from instant noodles, microwavable lunches to chips, candy bars & ice cream, to bottled refreshments. You name it, we probably have it! If not, leave a suggestion for us :)

Hot Drinks

We carry hot chocolate, tea packets & coffee for you to feel warmer after a brutal exam on a cold day. We also provide free hot water! (Club executives enjoy free tea & coffee).

Microwave

Tired of waiting in those long long ETLC microwave lines? Drop by our clubroom! We'll let you use ours, since we likely don't have a line up!

Lab Kits

If you are enrolled in ECE 210, 302, or 303, you and your lab partner might need to purchase a lab kit (with electrical parts) for your lab sessions. We also sell wire strippers, breadboards, and wires.

Textbooks

We have some textbooks that may relate to what you are studying right now. If you believe you need some extra resources, come check out our bookshelf! We provide daily textbook rentals.

Come Visit

If you have any questions for us, or just wanna make some friends, come visit our clubroom. We'll try our best to help with what you need, or just have a nice chat!

Our Events

FIRST YEAR NIGHT

First Year Night

We come together with Computer Engineering, Engineering Physics, and the ECE department to give first year students a better idea of who we are and what we do. Free food and drinks provided!

GEER WEEK FUN

GEER Week

Hosted by the Engineering Students' Society, GEER Week is a week filled with fun events, where each engineering discipline competes with each other (in a friendly way). This means a lot of partying!

ECE GRAD BANQUET

Grad Banquet

To send off our wonderful graduates of the year to embark on their new journeys, we host a annual, formal ECE graduation banquet to celebrate their success.

COOL MERCH SALES

Merch Sales

We come up with cool electrical engineering swag at least once a year. Checkout the Merch tab on our website to cop them, or sign up for our newsletter for any merch updates!
