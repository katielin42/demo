About Us

The electrical engineering club strives to create a dynamic, open-minded environment that nurtures the academic and creative growth of the electrical engineering student body as well as provide a welcoming place for students to meet and socialize. Additionally, we are the liaison between students and professors/faculty to collaborate on facilitating an education that harmonizes with both student interest and the many dynamic industries in Alberta.
